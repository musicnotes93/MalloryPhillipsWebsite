Meet Mallory Phillips

Welcome! I'm a professional violinist based in Fayetteville, NC, with over 10 years of experience teaching students of all ages and levels. I specialize in classical training but love incorporating a variety of styles—including fiddle, bluegrass, and pop—to keep lessons fun, creative, and tailored to each student. I hold a Master’s degree in violin performance, have performed with the North Carolina Symphony, and regularly play for weddings, private events, and community concerts. Whether you're looking for engaging violin lessons or elegant live music for your next event, I’m here to help bring the joy of music to you.
