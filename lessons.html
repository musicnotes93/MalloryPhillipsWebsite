My Philosophy

I believe music should be engaging and accessible. While I specialize in classical training, I love incorporating bluegrass, fiddle, and pop to keep students motivated. My goal is to build a strong technical foundation that allows students to play any style they love.

What I Offer

  • Suzuki & Traditional: A hybrid approach tailored to the student's learning style.
  • Orchestral Prep: Expert coaching for All-State, youth orchestra, and college auditions.
  • Technical Mastery: Focus on healthy posture, bow control, and strong fundamentals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you teach?

I specialize in teaching students from ages 5 through high school and college. My curriculum is designed to grow with the student, from early Suzuki fundamentals to advanced orchestral and audition preparation.

Do I need my own violin?

Yes, students will need their own instrument for daily practice. If you don't have one yet, I can recommend local shops in Fayetteville for quality rentals.

How does tuition work?

Tuition is paid monthly at the first lesson of each month. This reserves your specific weekly time slot in my studio schedule.

Where are lessons located?

Lessons are held at my home studio in Fayetteville, NC. Detailed directions are provided once the first lesson is scheduled.
